## 📋 RA1.1 AEE — Introduction to Regulatory Compliance

### 📊 Overview

Regulatory compliance in cybersecurity encompasses the frameworks, directives, and standards that organizations must adhere to in order to protect data, systems, and infrastructure. This comprehensive module covers the analysis of major European and Spanish regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR/RGPD), Network and Information Security Directives (NIS1, NIS2), the National Security Scheme (ENS), and key international standards. Understanding these frameworks is essential for implementing effective security governance and avoiding substantial penalties.

**Key Context:**
- 🎯 Spain and European regulatory landscape
- 🔐 Data protection and cybersecurity obligations
- 📊 Compliance-driven security measures
- 🛡️ Sector-specific requirements

## 📖 Key Concepts

> **Regulatory Compliance** — Adherence to laws, directives, regulations, and standards applicable to cybersecurity and data protection.

> **GDPR/RGPD** — General Data Protection Regulation; EU regulation on data protection and privacy for individuals within the Union.

> **NIS2** — Network and Information Security Directive 2; Enhanced cybersecurity requirements for operators and essential services.

> **ENS** — Esquema Nacional de Seguridad; Spain's national security scheme for information systems and data.

> **ISO/IEC 27001** — International standard for Information Security Management Systems (ISMS).

> **CISO** — Chief Information Security Officer; Executive responsible for information security and cybersecurity strategy.

> **Data Controller** — Entity determining purposes and means of personal data processing.

> **Data Processor** — Entity processing personal data on behalf of the controller.

## 📋 RA1.1 AEE — Introducción al Cumplimiento Normativo

### 📊 Descripción General

El cumplimiento normativo en ciberseguridad abarca los marcos, directivas y estándares que las organizaciones deben cumplir para proteger datos, sistemas e infraestructuras. Este módulo exhaustivo cubre el análisis de las principales regulaciones europeas y españolas, incluyendo el Reglamento General de Protección de Datos (RGPD), las Directivas de Seguridad de Redes e Información (NIS1, NIS2), el Esquema Nacional de Seguridad (ENS) e importantes estándares internacionales. Comprender estos marcos es esencial para implementar una gobernanza de seguridad efectiva y evitar sanciones sustanciales.

**Contexto Clave:**
- 🎯 Panorama regulatorio de España y Europa
- 🔐 Obligaciones de protección de datos y ciberseguridad
